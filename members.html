Alex

Alexander David Turner (born 6 January 1986) is the frontman and main songwriter of the Arctic Monkeys. He's released seven albums with the band. He has also recorded with his side project the Last Shadow Puppets and as a solo artist.

Matt

Matthew Helders (born 7 May 1986) is the drummer for the Arctic Monkeys but has also done vocal and song writing as well. He's released seven studio albums with the band.

Nick

Nicholas Edward O'Malley (born 5 July 1985) is the bass guitarist and backing vocalist for the Arctic Monkeys. Nick was a temporary replacement for the original bassist Andy Nicholson but ended up staying with the band and has released 6 studio albums with them.

Jamie

Jamie Robert Cook (born 8 July 1985) is a guitarist and songwriter for the Arctic Monkeys. He's recorded seven studio albums with the band.
